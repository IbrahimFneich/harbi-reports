◆ التوصية

Option 4 (Export modal) for the daily and monthly pages — it matches the editorial "military report" tone of the site and leaves room to add date-range and bulk exports later.

Option 1 (Single PDF) is the right call if you want to ship it this week with zero design overhead — you can always upgrade to Option 4 later.

Avoid Option 3 (chip bar) — it looks busy in the header alongside the stats row, and Option 5 (FAB) — it would collide with your existing spotlight floating button.
