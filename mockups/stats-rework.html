★ Stats Rework — Mockups mockups/stats-rework.html

What you're looking at

Three layout directions and three drill-down patterns for the الحصاد العام page. Same sample data in every mockup (lifted from your screenshot) so you compare design, not numbers. Click rows inside any mockup to see interactions.

Layouts

A1 — Minimal polish. Keep the current paper feel, promote the 3 big numbers to KPI cards, put targets + weapons side-by-side, add a soft bar inside each row so distribution is felt without a chart lib. No new deps.
A2 — Dashboard redux. 4 KPI cards with inline sparklines, horizontal bar chart for targets, donut for weapons, tables collapsed behind a toggle. Inline SVG only — no Chart.js.
A3 — Editorial / newspaper. Full masthead treatment, 3-column hero with a pull-quote for "أعلى يوم", 2-column ledger body. Matches the Amiri serif voice and prints beautifully.
Recommendation: A1 + a borrowed idea from A2 (inline row-bars). Biggest perceived upgrade with zero new deps.

Drill-down

B1 — Inline accordion. Click a row, it expands in-place to show the matching بيانات. Fastest; best for <50 items per bucket.
B2 — Side drawer. Click a row → panel slides in from the side with the filtered list grouped by day. Handles 1000+ items, keeps the stats context visible.
B3 — Navigate to filtered archive. Click a row → `archive.html?target=...` opens. Zero new rendering code; loses context.
Recommendation: B2 (side drawer). The "تصدّي" bucket alone has 1757 items — inline and modal both choke.
