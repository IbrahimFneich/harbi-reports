Drawer — panel slides in over the map

A 4th map button (under DOT/POLY) toggles a floating left-side drawer that overlays the map. Zero layout impact — map stays full-width everywhere.

Inline — panel below the map

No grid. The map is always full-width, and the legend + location list render as a horizontal row of cards underneath. Think "dashboard strip" instead of "side panel".
